# LLM Wiki — Karpathy

> Collected from: `https://karpathy.bearblog.dev/llm-wiki/`
> Collection date: 2026-06-12

## Summary

Andrej Karpathy proposes the "LLM Wiki" concept: a personal knowledge base where LLMs handle the heavy lifting of organizing, linking, and maintaining information. Humans provide raw materials; the LLM structures them.

## Key Points

1. **Compiled vs Retrieval**: LLM Wiki is "compiled" (LLM reads and rewrites) rather than "retrieval" (RAG from embeddings)
2. **Living document**: The wiki evolves as the LLM improves
3. **Low friction**: Throw things in, let the system sort them out
